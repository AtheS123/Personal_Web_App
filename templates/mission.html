Teaching Mission (2020-2021)

I served as a teaching missionary in the Colorado Fort Collins area. As part of this, I talked with many different people and worked together with several partners. Through my experiences here, I learned how to better communicate with people I don't know as well, as well as how to work together with different people to achieve a goal.

Service Mission (2021-2022)

I also worked as a service missionary in the Seattle area. My most notable experiences here are my work at a food bank known as the Bishop's Storehouse, and my work groundskeeping for the Seattle LDS temple. At the food bank, I got experience working at busy or stressful times, as well as with delegating work effectively so everything gets done. While groundskeeping, I developed a fine eye for details and some experience working with different plants and flowers.
